History

Care in the Community was established in 2020 to cater for the needs of a changing city. These changes reflect a more diverse community in a modern progressive Ireland. The aim of our service is social cohesion bettween old and new communities.

Right

Mission Statement

To help new communities integrate and navigate services whic help them in their daily lives. Our primary goal is that of integration and tolerance in a changing city.
